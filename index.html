Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the app and tap "Sign Up". You can register using your email address or Apple ID.

How do I verify my profile?

Go to your profile settings and follow the verification process. This helps ensure a safe community.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All data is encrypted and we follow strict privacy guidelines.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

Use the "Report" button on any profile or message. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

App Issues
